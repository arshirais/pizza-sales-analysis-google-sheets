## pizza-sales-analysis
Pizza Sales Analysis using SQL and Google Sheets
- **Project Objective:**

  1. Analyze one year of pizza sales data using SQL.
  2. Build a sales dashboard [Pizza Sales Dashboard (Google Sheets)](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1WQfSI3gSeaR4InMu1ScG9s3M0kwy_X1ExUNsTLP9GGg/edit?gid=1503815024#gid=1503815024) to visualize KPIs,
      sales trends, and   product performance. in Google Sheets to visualize KPIs, sales trends, and product performance.
  4. Generate actionable business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

- **Purpose of sales analytics:** Monitor sales performance, evaluate key business metrics, and identify customer purchasing patterns.

- **Importance of analyzing sales data:** Identify peak sales periods, evaluate product performance, and measure overall business growth through KPIs.

- **Role of the dashboard:** Help businesses track sales trends, analyze pizza category and size performance, identify top and bottom-selling products, and support strategic business decisions.
